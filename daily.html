Lesson: Everyday English You Actually Use

Situation
Meeting a coworker and talking about today’s work

Dialogue

  • A: Hey, how’s it going?
  • B: Pretty good. Just got busy this morning. How about you?
  • A: Same here. I’ve been in meetings all day.
  • B: Yeah, Mondays are always like that.
  • A: True. By the way, did you finish the report?
  • B: Almost. I’ll send it to you by the end of the day.
  • A: Perfect. Thanks, I really appreciate it.
  • B: No problem. Let me know if you need anything else.

Key Phrases You’ll Use All the Time

How’s it going?
→ A casual way to say “How are you?”

Pretty good. / Same here.
→ Simple, natural responses in daily conversation

I’ve been ~ all day.
→ Common way to talk about ongoing actions

By the way, ~
→ Smoothly changing the topic

By the end of the day
→ Very common in work and daily life

I really appreciate it.
→ Polite and natural gratitude

Let me know if you need anything else.
→ A standard closing phrase

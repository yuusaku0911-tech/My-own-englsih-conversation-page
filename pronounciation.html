Pronunciation Practice:
Speak Clearly and Naturally

Goal:
Improve your pronunciation so you sound clear, natural, and
easy to understand at work and in daily conversation.

Target Sounds & Rhythm

1. Sentence Stress（文の強弱）
I’ve been in meetings all day.

  • 強く読む：been / meetings / all day
  • 弱く読む：I’ve / in

👉 情報語を強く、機能語を弱く読むのが英語のリズム

2. Reduction（弱形）

How’s it going?
→ Howzit going?

I’ll send it to you.
→ I’ll send it t’you.

👉 実際の英語は「全部はっきり」発音しない

3. Linking（リンキング）

end of the day
→ end-ə-thə day

keep you posted
→ keep-ya posted

👉 単語はつながって聞こえる

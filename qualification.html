Pronunciation & Speaking
(For EIKEN & TOEIC)

Lesson 1｜Clear Final Sounds

Goal: Avoid losing points because of unclear pronunciation

Focus
Final consonants: t / d / k / p

Drill
Read each word clearly.

  • worked
  • asked
  • stopped
  • helped

Sentences
I worked late last night.
She asked me about the meeting.

Lesson 2｜Connected Speech for Listening

Goal: Improve TOEIC Part 3 & 4 listening accuracy

Focus
Sound linking in natural speech

Common Patterns

  • want to → wanna
  • going to → gonna

Practice

I’m going to take the test next week.
I want to improve my score.

Shadowing (3 times)

  1. Read and listen
  2. Listen without looking
  3. Speak with a slight delay
